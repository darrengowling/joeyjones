## üé• RECORDING TIPS

### Pacing:
- **Fast** - Speed up boring parts (form filling) by 2x in editing
- **Pause** - Hold on key moments (token copy, bid winning, standings) for 2-3 seconds

- Record the entire flow naturally

- Record alternate angles

- Cut out pauses and mistakes

## üé§ VOICEOVER RECORDING TIPS

### Equipment:
- **Good enough:** iPhone/Android voice memo app in quiet room
- **Better:** USB microphone ($30-50)
- **Best:** Professional mic ($100+)

### Recording:
1. Read script out loud 3 times to practice
2. Record in quiet room (turn off AC, close windows)
3. Phone 6 inches from mouth
4. Speak clearly but naturally (like talking to a friend)
5. Add enthusiasm - imagine you're telling your mate about something cool
6. Record 2-3 takes, pick the best

### Tone Examples:
- ‚ùå "Sport X is a platform for..." (too corporate)
- ‚úÖ "Love sports? Sport X is basically..." (conversational)
